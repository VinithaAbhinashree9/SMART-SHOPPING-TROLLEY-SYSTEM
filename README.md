# SMART-SHOPPING-TROLLEY-SYSTEM
An innovative Smart Shopping Trolley System that enables customers to scan products, generate bills automatically, and make digital payments without waiting in billing queues.

## Automated Billing Solution for Smart Retail Shopping

The Smart Shopping Trolley System is an innovative retail automation project designed to reduce billing queues and improve customer shopping experiences in malls and supermarkets.

This project enables customers to scan products while shopping and automatically generate bills in real time. Customers can complete payments digitally without waiting at billing counters, making shopping faster, smarter, and more convenient.

---

#  Problem Statement

In modern shopping malls and supermarkets, customers often spend a significant amount of time waiting in billing queues. Manual billing systems create delays, increase crowding, and reduce customer satisfaction.

The Smart Shopping Trolley System solves this problem by introducing an automated self-billing mechanism inside the shopping trolley itself.

---

#  Proposed Solution

The system allows customers to:

* Scan products using RFID or barcode technology
* View product details instantly
* Automatically update total bill amount
* Make payments through UPI, QR code, or card
* Exit the store without standing in long queues

#  Working Process

1. Customer enters the mall with a smart trolley
2. Products are scanned using RFID/barcode
3. Product information appears on display
4. Total bill updates automatically
5. Customer pays digitally
6. QR code or receipt is generated
7. Exit verification is completed
